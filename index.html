Privacy Policy

Effective date: December 11, 2025

Overview. The Kazakhstan Local Sites Enhancer (“Extension”) provides dark theme and page layout improvements for supported websites. All functionality runs locally in your browser.

Data Collection

The Extension does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data. No analytics, tracking scripts, or remote logging are used.

Local Processing

All operations — including visual adjustments and removal of non-essential page elements — occur entirely in your browser. No page content or user data is sent to external servers.

Permissions

The Extension may request browser permissions required to apply styling changes on supported websites. These permissions are used solely for local UI modifications and not for data collection.

Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use external APIs, SDKs, or services that would transmit data outside your device.

Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this Privacy Policy will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.
